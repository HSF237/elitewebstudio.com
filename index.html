Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to your common queries.

What is the delivery time for a website? ▶
The timeline depends on the complexity of your project. A basic website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while more complex projects can take 6-12 weeks. We'll provide you with a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.
Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance? ▶
Yes! All our packages include free support for a specified period. After that, we offer affordable maintenance plans to keep your website updated, secure, and running smoothly.
Can you help with SEO and digital marketing? ▶
Absolutely! We include basic SEO optimization in all our packages and offer advanced SEO and digital marketing services to help you reach your target audience and grow your online presence.
What if I need changes after the website is completed? ▶
We offer revisions during the development process and provide training so you can make basic updates yourself. For major changes, we're always available to help with our maintenance and support services.
